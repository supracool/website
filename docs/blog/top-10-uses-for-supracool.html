Top 10 Uses for SupraCool

Jan 2, 2023


Here are the top 10 uses for a cooling textile outdoors:

Headwear: Wearing a cooling sun hat to protect your head and face from the sun's rays.

  1. Sportswear: Keeping your body temperature down while training.
  2. Beachwear: Cooling swimsuit over-ups.
  3. Workwear: Cooling clothing for construction and outdoor workers.
  4. Middle Eastern -wear: Cooling abayas and thawbs for hot weather.
  5. Pets: Covering your dog with a cooling pad to keep them comfortable in the summer heat.
  6. Kids: Translucent cooling stroller to keep the heat out, but let light in.
  7. Tents: No more baking at summer music festivals!
  8. Placing a cooling cushion on a garden bench to make it more comfortable to sit on.
  9. Bags: Using a cooling tote bag to keep your groceries fresh on the way home from the market.

Bonus: Cooling cover for crops in the summer heat.
